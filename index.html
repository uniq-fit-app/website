Features Tailored for You

Overview

Uniq-fit, a unique gym for unique people. A personal gym built for everyone - with the specific needs of the trans community at its heart.

Gender-Aligned Profiles

Choose from masculinizing or feminizing plans, with three distinct profiles available for each option. Or choose to just stay in shape.

Tailored Weekly Plans

Pick shorter or longer workouts based on your personal schedule. Work your way up from beginner plans or start at an advanced level.

Personalized Workouts

We account for injuries, training level, and even your current gender- affirming care when putting together your plan.

Progress & Body Tracking

Track your reps, measurements, and personal progress easily in the app. We’ll make sure to remind you so you don’t forget!

The People Behind Uniq-Fit

Kate Costa (she/they)

Co-Founder & Product Lead

Kate has worked as a designer and developer for over a decade. While considering her transition, she turned to fitness with Raul’s help to ease gender dysphoria, gaining greater control over her body and self-image. A year later, they co-founded Uniq-Fit to help others build that same confidence through fitness.

Raul San Bartolome (he/him)

Co-Founder & Fitness Lead

Raul has worked as a personal trainer for over ten years. He has trained a diverse set of clients, coached and competed in lower-level basketball, and instructed rock climbing. After seeing the impact fitness had on Kate’s transition, he co-founded Uniq-Fit to provide that same support to others on their journey.
